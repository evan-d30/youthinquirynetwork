Empowering High Schoolers to become

At the Youth Inquiry Network, our mission is simple yet profound: to empower high school students like you to become confident researchers, critical thinkers, and effective communicators.

Join Us Program Registration

Our Pillars

Inclusivity

Inclusivity is foundational to creating an environment where all students, regardless of their background or resources, feel welcomed, valued, and supported. We acknowledge the importance of accessibility and diversity within the STEM field and strive to ensure that every high school student has the opportunity to participate, learn, and contribute.

Innovation

Innovation is at the core of advancing the STEM fields, promoting the pursuit of new knowledge, technologies, and solutions to complex problems. Our commitment is to encourage and empower students to exercise creative thinking, problem-solving, and the exploration of uncharted territories in research.

Collaboration

Collaboration is the power of collective effort and shared knowledge in achieving greater outcomes than what could be accomplished individually hence why we aim to build an inclusive community. We promote the importance of teamwork, communication, and the exchange of ideas among students, mentors, and the broader scientific community.

Our Mission Statement

At the Youth Inquiry Network, our mission is simple yet profound: to empower high school students like you to become confident researchers, critical thinkers, and effective communicators. We believe that every student has the potential to make meaningful contributions to the world through research and scholarly inquiry. However, our commitment doesn't stop there. We recognize the lingering financial barriers that often stand between young researchers and their research journey which is why we are also committed to providing accessible and affordable solutions for research publication. It's our aim to ensure that students have the right training and guidance to share and conduct research without breaking the bank.

Are you a university student passionate about science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM)? The Youth Inquiry Network is looking for YOU! Become a mentor and empower high school students to become confident researchers and effective communicators.

Develop Your Skills

This is an incredible opportunity to develop your own skills as well. You'll gain valuable experience in mentorship, research guidance, and education, all while strengthening your resume. Upon completing the mentorship program, you'll receive a certificate in recognition of your contribution and the expertise you've gained..

Leave a Lasting Legacy

Most importantly, you'll have a tangible impact on the lives of your students. By helping them conduct and publish original research, you'll be leaving a lasting legacy and fostering the next generation of scientific discovery.
